The Isle of Skye

The Isle of Skye is the largest and northernmost of the major islands in the Inner Hebrides of Scotland. Highland Hiking is Free for everyone, just come with your nice pair of trainers!
Simply enjoy the greatest nature and find yourself where you are belonged.
